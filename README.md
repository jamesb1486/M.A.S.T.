M.A.S.T. – Marine Alignment & Strain Tracker

Overview

M.A.S.T. (Marine Alignment & Strain Tracker) is a patent pending autonomous dock monitoring and stabilization system designed to improve the safety, reliability, and longevity of floating dock installations.

The system continuously monitors dock alignment, cable tension, environmental conditions, and structural movement. By analyzing data from multiple distributed sensors, M.A.S.T. determines when corrective action is required and can automatically adjust cable tension to maintain proper dock alignment as water levels, wind, waves, and daily environmental conditions change.

This project combines embedded systems, industrial automation, software engineering, and hardware design into a single integrated platform.

⸻

Project Goals

* Monitor dock alignment in real time
* Measure cable tension continuously
* Track environmental conditions affecting dock movement
* Automate corrective adjustments when safe and necessary
* Provide reliable long term monitoring with minimal maintenance
* Create a scalable platform suitable for residential and commercial dock installations
